Our Story

Our Story | ታሪካችን

10+ Years of Excellence

At Mag Garment, we weave Ethiopian heritage into every thread. Our designs draw from the ancient art of tibeb — the hand-embroidered borders that have defined habesha fashion for centuries. Each piece is crafted by skilled artisans in Addis Ababa using premium Ethiopian cotton, blending tradition with contemporary style for Ethiopians everywhere.
